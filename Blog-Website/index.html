Key Points:

  • The Year 2038 problem is related to the representation of time in computer systems.
  • It is caused by the use of a 32-bit signed integer to represent time in seconds since the Unix epoch.
  • The Year 2038 problem will occur on January 19, 2038, when the 32-bit signed integer overflows and resets to a negative value.
